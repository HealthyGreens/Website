Frequently Asked Questions















Managing your plants



How do I add a plant?

Once you are logged in, hover over the "Plants" dropdown in the header and simply click "Add Plant". There you will fill out the info about your plant including name, watering frequencies, times and background info. Once you submit that form that plant will be registered to your account.

How do I view my plants?

Assuming you are logged in whenever you visit back to the home page your plants will be listed by frequency. Meaning plants that need to be watered on a specific date/time you input will show up first based on close that date is.

How do I edit/remove a plant from my account?

If you are logged in hover over the "Plants" dropdown in the header and click "Edit Plant". From there you will se a list of all your plants along with all of their info. Edit any of the textboxes to include the new information and click the update button on the right side of the page. If you want to delete a plant from your account then find the corresponding plant and click the red Delete button on the right side of the page.
