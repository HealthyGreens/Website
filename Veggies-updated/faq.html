Managing your plants



What is Healthy Greens?

Healthy Greens is an indie software program made to help people keep track of their plants.

Why is Healthy Greens so useful?

There really isn't another free to use program designed to easily keep track of your plants. With Easy Greens you have an easy way to keep track of your plant's watering days, times, frequencies and more.
